About Us

A New York Classic For Almost 30 Years Serafina’s unique combination of a casual-cool setting, charming service, and its creative twist on homestyle Italian fare has kept both locals and a celebrity-studded clientele coming back for more. Quite simply, Serafina has become one of the world’s favorite restaurants.

Our commitment to quality ingredients and authentic recipes ensures every bite is a taste of Italy. From our handcrafted pizzas to our delicious pasta dishes, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.
